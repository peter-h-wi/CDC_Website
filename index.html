1. Hwanung - The Son of the Lord of Heaven

The story starts from introducing Hwanung, who will be the father of Dangun, the founder of Gojoseon.
Once upon a time, there was Hwanung, who was a son of the lord of heaven.
He was interested in the human world, so he came down to the earth.
